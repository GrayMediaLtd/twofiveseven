our furniture

our furniture will be custom made with covid design considerations from materials we’ve prevented from going to landfill

our furniture is super sustainable:

  • designtree are creating our custom furniture range from materials salvaged from the old office fitout previously in the space
  • meaning we’re dramatically reducing waste and our carbon footprint - something we are striving for across all of our construction and development

and we’re developing it to suit our new work environment:

  • we’ve partnered with local research startup stickybeak to define the fast developing new needs of local office users
  • our desks offer sit and stand positions, and come with an optional full privacy guard so you are able to comfortably video call and safely physical distance at your desk
  • we’re also providing home desk rental. we think having the same desks at both office and home will make the transitions a whole lot easier

our story

twofiveseven is being led by spacelamp - a community building, spacemaking and impact-focused initiative making places for good

we have over a decade of experience creating spaces in the netherlands, austria and aotearoa. most recently we’ve created and developed spaces and community hubs in whangārei:

  • oneonesix is a project by company of giants, with support from spacelamp, turning an unused former church in the centre of whangārei into a thriving hub for community events, theatre, music and art. the space now has over 13,000 visitors annually, is home to beagle community radio, small coffee bar, and hosts a huge and diverse range of events throughout the year
  • wood was an unused warehouse space and we were a part of turning it into a space that has supported over 20 community organisations, startups and impact organisations. today it is home to forward, ano reuse again, sew good, the wholefood hub, and whare bike

in 2019 we moved our focus to whanganui-a-tara, to create twofiveseven

spacelamp is certified carbon positive for 2019 by ekos
